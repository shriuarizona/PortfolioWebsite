I'm Parul Sancheti

Photographs are invaluable treasures, preserved by previous generations and passed on to us. The feel, the touch, the emotion makes us re-live those special moments of our lives. I realised my passion for photography in class 8 and from there on, the more I explored the art of photography, the deeper I got drawn into it. Soon, I began endeavouring to grasp every piece of information I could, from photographers, whether they were family, friends or even just casual acquaintances. I soon got my own equipment, and delved deeper into the subject by implementing what I had learnt through my research. I soon got the opportunity to put my knowledge to the test, when my mother's home bakery turned commercial. I began photographing her products from a commercial perspective and set up her social media platforms. I learned that a good product photograph would not just sell the product but would also sell the good feeling that complements the delicious designer cakes. Soon, the business started making giant strides and we had interested customers regularly viewing the business's social media pages to stay updated about our products.
